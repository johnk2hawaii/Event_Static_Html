Founder, Cosmic Space Warrior, Traveller, And Heart Breaker

Dr. John Koblanski

Dr. John Koblanski began his academic journey with a joint major in mathematics and physics and a minor in chemistry, followed by a master’s degree in theoretical physics from the University of Northern British Columbia. After an initial career transition into investment advising, he returned to academia to complete a Ph.D. in particle physics at the University of Hawai‘i, subsequently holding a postdoctoral research position. Throughout his career, spanning both academia and the private sector, Dr. Koblanski has been a pioneer in applying machine learning to fundamental physics. He has authored several highly cited publications in leading scientific journals, contributed to the design and testing of advanced detector systems, and co-founded Ultralytics LLC—now the world’s leading platform for computer vision technologies.
